Ingredients

  • 1 once jar prego, traditional italian sauce
  • 6 each uncooked lasagna noodles
  • 1 container ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese

Directions

  1. Spread about 1 cup pasta sauce in 2 quart shallow baking dish. Top with 3 uncooked noodles, ricotta cheese.

    Top with remaining 3 uncooked noodels and remaining pasta sauce. Cover.

  2. Bake at 375 degrees for 1 hour uncover and top with remianing mozzarella cheese. Let stand 5 minutes

  3. Enjoy!
